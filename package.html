Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our professional development services and packages.

What's included in the support period?

Support includes bug fixes, minor adjustments, technical assistance, and guidance on using your new website. Major feature additions are quoted separately.

Can I customize a package to fit my needs?

Absolutely. Each package can be tailored to your specific requirements. Contact me to discuss a custom solution that fits your business needs and budget.

What's the typical project timeline?

Timelines vary by project complexity. Shopify stores typically take 2-4 weeks, WordPress (ACF) projects 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline in your proposal.

Do you provide hosting and domain setup?

I can assist with hosting recommendations and setup. Hosting costs are separate and depend on your requirements. I'll guide you through the process.

What happens after the support period ends?

After the included support period, I offer ongoing maintenance packages or hourly support. You'll have full access to your website and can choose your preferred support model.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients globally. Based in Bangkok, I've successfully delivered projects for businesses in multiple countries. Communication is conducted in English via email, video calls, and project management tools.

Need a Custom Enterprise Solution?

Every business is unique. Let's discuss a tailored solution designed specifically for your requirements, scale, and growth objectives. I'll provide a detailed proposal with architecture, timeline, and deliverables.
